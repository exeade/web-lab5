Featured Coffee of the Month:

"Colombian Supremo - September Special"

This month, we're highlighting our rich and smooth Colombian Supremo beans! Grown high in the Andean mountains, these beans are known for their well-balanced flavor, with notes of caramel, chocolate, and a hint of citrus. The medium body and mild acidity make it a versatile coffee that's perfect for any time of day. Whether you enjoy it as a strong espresso or a smooth pour-over, our Colombian Supremo will make every sip a treat.

Brewing Tips:

Make the most of your coffee with these brewing tips tailored to different methods:

  • Pour-Over:
    1. Grind: Medium-fine grind for a balanced extraction.
    2. Water Temp: 195°-205°F (90°-96°C).
    3. Ratio: Use 1g of coffee for every 16g of water for optimal flavor.
    4. Tip: Pour water slowly in circular motions to evenly saturate the grounds.
  • Espresso:
    1. Grind: Fine grind for a concentrated shot.
    2. Water Temp: 190°-195°F (88°-91°C).
    3. Ratio: Use 18g of coffee for a double shot (about 60ml).
    4. Tip: Tamp the coffee evenly to ensure even extraction and a rich crema.
  • French Press:
    1. Grind: Coarse grind for a smoother, less bitter taste.
    2. Water Temp: 200°F (93°C).
    3. Ratio: 1:15 coffee-to-water ratio for a rich brew.
    4. Tip: After 4 minutes of steeping, stir gently and press the plunger slowly to avoid over-extraction.
